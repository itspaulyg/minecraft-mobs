Creeper

HitPoints:

20 ( x 10)

Spawn

  • Creepers spawn in the Overworld on solid blocks
  • Spawn with a light level of 7 or less

Drops

  • 5 exp if killed by player or tamed wolf
  • 0-2 gunpowder (up to 5 with Looting)
  • 1 random music disc (excluding Pigstep), if killed by a Skeleton
  • 1 creeper head if killed by a charged creeper
